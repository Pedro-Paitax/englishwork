Human Body: Our Inner World

Review From Lucas Alexandre

The Documentary

In the documentary "Human Body: Our Inner World", being more precise on the episode analyzed, or number 5, we can gain a glimpse of how our senses work (sight, smell, hearing, touch, taste and proprioception) besides showing us the career of a deaf singer and a pair of ice skaters, thus exemplifying in an easy to understand way the impact of the senses on people's lives and work.

Points

The positive point of this episode, or of the whole series, is how it managed to remain easy to understand for lay people in the medical field thanks to the simple explanation and the moderate explanation pace, thus managing to captivate the viewer's attention in a sensational way. Although only 2 people worked on this series, you can still see the effort and care that went into the documentary.
