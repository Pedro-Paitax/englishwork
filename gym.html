Gym At Home?

Interview With Professor Fernando

Interview With Fernando

Supposing that the reader is going through a boring Sunday where he wants to practice a sport or physical activity, even if he doesn't have the money or the physique to do so, what suggestion would you give to this person, teacher?

"Normally all physical activity must be performed for two reasons, one being the will to do it / leisure and the other for necessity, that is, there are people with the need to practice sports or exercise because of their health. Answering your question, I would normally indicate a park because you can go with the family and also have a good leisure time and several forms of exercise, such as, for example, a walk, which is the most practical and cheap current exercise besides, with your evolution, you can start running instead of walking."
